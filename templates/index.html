Mice Tumor Dashboard

The purpose of this dashboard is to analyze and display valuable insights from data using matplotlib and Pandas. The methods used include generating summary statistics, bar and pie charts, line plots and scatter plots with regression and Pearson r values, calculation of quartiles, IQR, and finding outliers.
  • Summary Statistics
  • Data Points per Drug Regimen
  • Distribution of Mice (Male vs. Female)
  • Four Drug Treatments (IQR) - Boxplot
  • Tumor Volume Over Time (Capomulin)
  • Average Tumor Volume vs Weight (Capomulin) Scatterplot + Linear Regression
